World Environment Day is celebrated annually on June 5th to raise awareness and encourage action for environmental protection. It's the United Nations' principal vehicle for promoting environmental awareness and action globally. The day is a platform for governments, businesses, communities, and individuals to address pressing environmental challenges and drive sustainable change.
Water pollution is the contamination of water bodies by harmful substances, rendering them unsafe for human use and detrimental to aquatic ecosystems. This contamination can result from both natural and human activities, with human actions being the primary driver in most cases. Common pollutants include industrial and agricultural waste, sewage, oil spills, and improper disposal of waste. The consequences of water pollution are far-reaching, impacting human health, ecosystems, and the environment.
